Our Licensed Acupuncturists

Dr. Y.D. Anton Lin, L.Ac., PhD and Dr. Y.H. Mirada Wang, L.Ac. are both licensed acupuncturists in California, each bringing extensive training and expertise in Traditional Chinese Medicine.

With strong skills in acupuncture and deep backgrounds in herbal medicine, our practitioners believe acupuncture helps activate the body's natural physical and chemical healing responses. Through years of study and practice, they have found that Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) offers a safe and effective way to improve health with minimal side effects.

TCM includes acupuncture along with other traditional therapies such as herbal medicine, cupping, tuina, and moxibustion, which work well alongside Western medicine. With over 10 years of practice since founding the clinic in 2016, our team is dedicated to bringing effective TCM treatments to patients throughout the greater Los Angeles area.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during a treatment?

During your initial visit, we conduct a thorough consultation where you'll discuss your health concerns and history. Using traditional Chinese medicine diagnostic techniques, your practitioner will develop a personalized treatment plan. A typical session may include acupuncture, massage, herbal prescriptions, and self-care guidance tailored to your needs.

Will the needles hurt?

Most patients experience minimal to no discomfort. Acupuncture needles are hair-thinâ€”much smaller than hypodermic needles used for injections. While sensitivity varies by person and treatment point, most first-time patients are pleasantly surprised by how comfortable the experience is.

How long until I see improvement?

Results vary based on your condition, overall health, and treatment frequency. Chronic conditions typically require more sessions than acute issues. Your active participation in self-careâ€”including recommended exercises, dietary changes, and self-acupressureâ€”significantly influences your recovery timeline and long-term wellness.

Are the needles safe?

Absolutely. We use only sterile, individually packaged, single-use needles that are safely disposed of after each treatment. Your safety is our top priority.

Are there any side effects?

Acupuncture is generally very safe with minimal side effects. Occasionally, patients may experience minor redness, light bruising, or slight soreness at needle sites. Serious side effects are extremely rare.

What conditions can acupuncture treat?

Research shows acupuncture effectively treats chronic pain (back, neck, arthritis, fibromyalgia), headaches, post-surgical pain, sinusitis, asthma, menstrual issues, menopausal symptoms, anxiety, and urological problems. Our practitioners work with a wide range of conditions using time-tested traditional methods.
